About

Schimmelbusch Law Group PC serves clients in Oregon and Washington with matters related to estate planning and administration, business succession planning, and charitable planned giving.

Erik S. Schimmelbusch

Fellow, American College of Trust and Estate Counsel (ACTEC). Rated AV Preeminent by Martindale-Hubbell. Selected as an Oregon “Super Lawyer” multiple years.

Practice Highlights

  • Traditional wills and trusts; estate and gift tax planning/reporting.
  • Business succession planning for family and closely held businesses.
  • Charitable gift planning: CRTs, CLTs, life estate gifts, gifts of practices.
  • Irrevocable life insurance trusts and intentionally defective grantor trusts.
  • Post-mortem administration of estates and trusts (Forms 706/709; OR/WA returns).

Awards & Activities

  • ACTEC Fellow; OSB Estate Planning & Administration Section, past Chair.
  • Super Lawyers (2012–2021). Five Star Wealth Manager (2011–2014).
  • Speaker and author on estate, tax, and charitable planning topics.

Education & Admissions

  • B.A., Humanities, Brigham Young University.
  • J.D. and LL.M. (Business & Taxation), McGeorge School of Law.
  • Oregon (1997), Washington (2010); California (inactive).
